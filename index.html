Analysis

Using Python, I found the distribution of letters for the words officially recognized by Wordle. We can clearly see that some letters are more likely to occur than others; the most common letters are S, E, A, O, and R, while the least common are Q, J, X, Z, and V. It is worth noting that these numbers do not account for duplicates - for example, the letter L is overrepresented as it often appears twice in the same word and guessing T would be more likely to yield success.

Based on these frequencies, your 'best' first guess would likely be one of the following: SOARE, ROATE, SALET, REAST, or RAISE. If you have no interest in winning, feel free try words such as QAJAQ, IMMIX, or JAZZY!
